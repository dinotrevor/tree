Chapter 1

A strange smell filled the air, I was just finishing my last cigarette as the clock strikes 7 p.m. . The sun is down and Christmas is in 2 days. I go inside my house after my cigarette to get my wallet and keys when my phone starts ringing. It was my wife Hollie. "Hey honey I'll be home late tonight, there is a lot of paper work." She said. "Okay, be careful and call me when you can I love you bye." I replied. I hung up and walked out the door. I needed to get a few items at store, but before I started the car my boss Rudy texts me and says turn on the news as soon as possible. I went back inside my house disregarding the store. I immediately texted my wife and told her to come home as soon as possible. I turn on the news, and my phone rings it's Rudy. "Did you hear about the giant animals Trevor?" He asked. "Yes I just turned on the news, my god sir I'm gonna have to call you back." I said and hung up. I then called Robert and Tyler and told them to come over as soon as possible. Dinner is starting as Hollie,Robert,Tyler,Sara and Amanda arrive. Hollie and I kiss as we both look down at her stomach. She is due with twins here in a couple months. We all gathered around the TV to hear the breaking news. The news reporter was in the middle of a story about the newest global predicaments involving giant animals slaughtering everything in their path. " All around every state in the country, farmers and citizens have become victims of an onslaught of giant animals. 353 total deaths in America , 604 worldwide, we will have more coverage of the story progresses.". Tyler asked us all "Can you believe what is going on?". Robert replied "This is madness. What could have caused this?". Me and Hollie get on the web to see more information on the chaos. There was 1100 deaths in Asia, and 700 deaths from Europe and North America. I called my boss Rudy to let him know my findings. "Sir there have been about 2,000 deaths worldwide, and it has been raining across the globe for 24 hours." He replied with urgency " Grab arms and stay safe you must still be present to work as you will be working on this case don't be late tomorrow!". My job was the most important thing to me after Hollie. The importance of having a good life for my family is priority. "Hollie I have to work tomorrow, I'll try to get off early." I said. I could tell she was now sad. Robert spoke up "Dude it's Christmas Eve tomorrow." The night has started to come to a close, after Robert, Tyler, Sarah, and Amanda went home Hollie and I went to bed. It was Christmas Eve tomorrow and I was about to help solve this giant animal case. It is 6 a.m. Hollie has coffee ready for me. She asks "Why do you have to work today?" I replied "They want me to investigate the giant animals around the globe." I kiss Hollie and say goodbye. I stop and get a bite to eat before work. The guy at McDonald's said their supply of beef has been cut off overnight. I get two hash browns and head to work. I arrived at the Renouter the same time my boss Rudy did. I shake his hand and greet him in the parking lot as we made our way inside.
